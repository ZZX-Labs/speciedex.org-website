Responsible Security Disclosure

Please do not publicly disclose security vulnerabilities until they have been investigated and, where appropriate, corrected.

Include reproduction steps, affected versions, proof-of-concept information, and supporting evidence whenever possible.

Security Email: security@speciedex.org
