Security

Responsible Security Disclosure

Please do not publicly disclose security vulnerabilities until they have been investigated and, where appropriate, corrected.

Include reproduction steps, affected versions, proof-of-concept information, potential impact, and supporting evidence whenever possible.

Security Contact

Send responsible security disclosures to security@speciedex.org.

Response Expectations

Response times depend on the nature, urgency, and complexity of each inquiry.

General Questions

Normally reviewed within several business days, subject to project workload and availability.

Security Reports

Reviewed as quickly as practical based on severity, reproducibility, potential impact, and affected systems.

Partnership Requests

Response times vary depending on the scope and complexity of the proposed collaboration.

Before Contacting Us

Please review the documentation, frequently asked questions, release notes, legal notices, and available project resources before submitting a request.
