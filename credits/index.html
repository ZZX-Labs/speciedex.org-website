This page recognizes the people, projects, databases, institutions, organizations, technologies, and communities whose work informed, inspired, enabled, or supported the creation of Speciedex.

Dedication

To Those Who Made It Possible

Speciedex is dedicated to the countless wildlife filmmakers, scientists, conservationists, naturalists, explorers, educators, artists, archivists, engineers, programmers, and volunteers who made it possible for others to see, study, understand, and protect the natural world.

It is dedicated to every person who stood in a forest, crossed a desert, climbed a mountain, entered a cave, descended beneath the ocean, worked through the night in a laboratory, opened a forgotten collection drawer, restored a damaged film, digitized an aging book, wrote an open-source library, or defended a threatened habitat so that knowledge would survive.
