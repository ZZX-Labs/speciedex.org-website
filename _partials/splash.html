The Open Species Index

Speciedex is an open, Bitcoin-powered biodiversity infrastructure project building a permanent, freely accessible index of Earth's known species. By continuously reconciling authoritative taxonomic providers into a unified canonical archive, Speciedex enables researchers, conservationists, educators, developers, governments, and the public to discover, analyze, preserve, and build upon global biodiversity knowledge.
