What is Speciedex?

Speciedex is an open-source, Bitcoin-native biodiversity infrastructure project designed to catalogue, organize, reference, and preserve information relating to every known species on Earth. It combines modern taxonomy, scientific metadata, distributed infrastructure, and open documentation into a freely available platform for research, education, conservation, and software development.

The project consists of four major components: Speciedex, SpeciedexCore, SpeciedexExplorer, and SpeciedexNet.
